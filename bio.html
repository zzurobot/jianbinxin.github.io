Biography

Yiming Wan received the Ph.D. degree in Automatic Control from Tsinghua University in 2013. From 2013 to 2016, he was a postdoc researcher at Delft Center for Systems and Control, Delft University of Technology, The Netherlands. During 2016-2018, he was a Research Associate at Department of Chemical Engineering, Massachusetts Institute of Technology, the United States. Since February 2018, he has been an Associate Professor in the School of Artificial Intelligence and Automation, Huazhong University of Science and Technology, Wuhan, China. His research interests include fault-tolerant control, industrial data analytics, state and parameter estimation, and model predictive control.
